The Fit

This is for you if

  • You already have revenue and want leverage, not launches
  • You're building in luxury, wellness, beauty, or regulated industries
  • You can feel the brand is bigger than the business strategy around it
  • You want a strategist, not a cheerleader
  • You're ready to invest at a level that changes how you think

This is not for you if

  • You haven't started yet and want a coach to tell you what to do
  • You're looking for social media management or execution
  • You want weekly accountability rather than strategy
  • You're not ready to make decisions between sessions
  • You expect a guarantee instead of a partnership
